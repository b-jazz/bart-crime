Bart Crimes

Making Public Informaion Public

In spite of a recent initiative to "increase public's access to BART crime data", they have in fact been reducing the amount of crime information they publish information because fear of racial sterotyping . While I agree with the notion that i would be a shame if racial biases are formed for any reason, I am of the firm belief that it is not BART's job to hide information from us. Nor is it their right.

BART has reduced their daily crime updates to a mailing list which requires approval. This information is scraped from those daily emails and posted. I only have emails back to July 22, 2017. If anyone has more, please reach out to me.

I created this site while sitting on BART for my daily commute back and forth Fremont to San Francisco. I've seen a lot of crazy things on that commute, and I think security is in serious need of some attention. Particularly gate security. I'd venture to say a huge percent of the negative press BART is getting is by the same people I see hopping the fare gate on daily basis. Nothing is ever done about it.

I love BART, but I've had it with the overly-aggressive begging, bodily fluids, needles, and I see on a daily basis.

Follow @bart_crimes on Twitter to receive each incident in your feed.
